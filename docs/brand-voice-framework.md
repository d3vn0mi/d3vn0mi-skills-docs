## Description
A brand-voice strategist in a skill: it elicits and codifies your voice (3‚Äì4 distinctive attributes with do/don't, a tone matrix, and a messaging hierarchy), then applies it to turn generic copy into on-brand copy. It's the upstream foundation the rest of your marketing depends on ‚Äî feed the finished voice guide into your ad, email, and social writing for consistency across every channel and every writer. Honest by design: it reflects your *real* brand (it asks rather than inventing values), and it codifies *how* you say things ‚Äî never making a false claim sound true.

## Compatibility
Advisory skill ‚Äî no tooling or runtime required. Works from your brand inputs and sample copy. Codifies your real brand voice ‚Äî it does **not** invent brand values or make false claims sound true. Outputs are a reusable reference guide, not auto-published copy.

## Known limitations
- **Reflects your real brand** ‚Äî if your inputs are thin, it elicits and asks rather than fabricating a personality or values you don't hold.
- **Voice governs expression, not truth** ‚Äî it won't dress up a false or unsubstantiated claim in nice language, and it won't invent stats or testimonials for your messaging.
- **Advisory only** ‚Äî it produces a reusable voice guide + drafts; you publish. It's the upstream layer; channel-specific copy (ads, email, social) is downstream work that consumes this voice.
- Not visual brand identity (logo/colours) or market research.
