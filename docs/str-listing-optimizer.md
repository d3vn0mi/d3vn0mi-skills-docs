# str-listing-optimizer
> Turn a flat short-term-rental listing into a booking machine — honestly.

## Description
A listing consultant for short-term-rental hosts: it rewrites your title and description, prioritizes the amenities guests actually filter for, gives you a photo brief, and lays out the legitimate levers that influence OTA search ranking — across Airbnb, Vrbo, and Booking.com. Works from your real property details and produces ready-to-paste copy plus a prioritized action list. Honest by design: it never invents amenities you don't have, never fakes distances or badges, and never promises a #1 rank.

## Compatibility
Advisory skill — no tooling or runtime required. Works from your property details. Produces listing copy only — **does not post, fabricate amenities/reviews, or guarantee ranking.** Platform rules + search algorithms change — verify current policies on the platform.

## Keywords
Airbnb listing optimization, STR listing, vacation rental listing, Airbnb title, listing description, Airbnb SEO, OTA ranking, Booking.com listing, Vrbo, short-term rental marketing, amenities, listing photos, Airbnb search ranking, get more bookings, Superhost, host

## Sample input
> "Here's my Airbnb listing for a 1-bed flat near a park: title 'Nice apartment in the city centre', a generic description, and a list of what we actually have. Optimize it and tell me how to rank better."

## Sample output
*(Illustrative shape — honest throughout.)*
- **Title:** rewritten to lead with the genuine standout (e.g. the park view or the walkable location), within the platform's character norms — only features the flat truly has.
- **Description:** restructured into the arc — hook → the space → who it's for → honest neighbourhood/proximity → logistics — scannable, no exaggerated distances.
- **Amenities + photos:** the high-impact filterable amenities you *actually* have, surfaced first; a cover-photo choice + a room-by-room shot list with an honest-representation rule (no fish-eye distortion, no hiding defects).
- **Ranking actions:** legitimate levers — listing completeness, fast response/acceptance, reviews, fair pricing, up-to-date calendar — with the honest caveat that the algorithm is proprietary, changes, and **no one can guarantee a specific rank.**

## Known limitations
- **Advisory only** — it drafts copy; you post it. It will not post, fabricate amenities/reviews, or guarantee bookings.
- **Truthful listings only** — it refuses inventing amenities you lack, false distances, fake badges/reviews, false accessibility claims, and deceptive photo tactics.
- **No rank guarantees** — OTA algorithms are proprietary and change; it frames ranking factors as the known public levers and tells you to verify current platform guidance.
- **Doesn't shoot your photos** — it gives the brief; you (or a photographer) take honest photos that match reality.
- Not dynamic pricing or review-response (see other skills); not legal/regulatory advice on operating an STR.
