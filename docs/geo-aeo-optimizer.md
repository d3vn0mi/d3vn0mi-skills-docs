# geo-aeo-optimizer
> Get your content cited by AI answer engines — Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity — the white-hat way.

## Description
A Generative / Answer Engine Optimization (GEO/AEO) consultant in a skill. It audits your content and gives concrete, white-hat recommendations to improve your odds of being surfaced and cited by AI answer engines: how the engines retrieve and cite sources, how to structure content for extraction, how to build genuine entity authority (E-E-A-T + schema), how the major engines differ, and how to measure AI visibility honestly. Works from your real content and topic; produces an AEO audit + a prioritized action list. White-hat by design — it never manipulates engines, fabricates authority, or guarantees a ranking.

## Compatibility
Advisory skill — no tooling or runtime required. **White-hat optimization only — it does not manipulate AI engines or guarantee citations/ranking.** AI search engines change fast, so it frames everything verify-current — confirm current behavior and guidance on each platform.

## Keywords
GEO, generative engine optimization, AEO, answer engine optimization, AI SEO, get cited by AI, Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT search, Perplexity, AI search visibility, E-E-A-T, schema markup, structured data, brand entity, content optimization, LLM citations, marketing

## Sample input
> "Here's a blog post and our site. We want to show up when people ask AI assistants about our topic. Audit it and tell me what to change — and how the different engines differ."

## Sample output
*(Illustrative shape — white-hat, verify-current throughout.)*
- **Fundamentals:** how answer engines retrieve → ground → cite, and where your content currently falls short (no fabricated "algorithm" — the known, public mechanics).
- **Content structure:** lead each section with a direct answer, question-based headings, scannable self-contained passages an engine can lift cleanly, statistics with *real* sources.
- **Entity authority:** E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust — Google's framework) with *real* author credentials; correct schema.org types (Article, FAQPage, Organization, Person, BreadcrumbList) framed as aiding eligibility, not guaranteeing a citation.
- **Engine differences + measurement:** what Google AI Overviews vs ChatGPT Search vs Perplexity tend to favor (verify-current), the right crawler/robots settings, and an honest measurement plan — what you can track and what has no public API.

## Known limitations
- **White-hat only** — it refuses prompt-injection/manipulation of AI summaries, fabricated authority or credentials, cloaking, and publishing false content to win citations.
- **No guarantees** — AI engines are proprietary and change fast; it gives the known levers framed verify-current and will not promise a citation, an AI Overview spot, or a rank.
- **Accuracy first** — content must be true; it won't help dress up unsupported claims.
- **Advisory only** — it audits and recommends; you implement. Not classic technical-SEO depth, not a rank-tracking tool, not paid ads.
