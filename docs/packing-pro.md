# packing-pro
> Pack smarter — a trip-tailored packing list, a capsule wardrobe, and carry-on technique, without the overpacking.

## Description
A packing strategist in a skill. From your trip details — destination climate, length, activities, trip type, and luggage limits — it builds a tailored packing list, plans a mix-and-match capsule wardrobe, sorts what goes in your carry-on vs checked bag, and walks you through space-saving packing techniques and a don't-forget essentials checklist. It stays strictly in the packing lane: it helps you decide *what* to pack and *how*, and it points you to the right official source for anything regulatory — it does not give visa, entry, health, safety, or price advice.

## Compatibility
Advisory skill — no tooling or runtime required. Works from your trip details and produces a tailored packing list + packing strategy. **Packing advice only — it does not advise on visas/entry, health, safety, or prices (use official sources), and baggage allowances + liquid/prohibited rules vary by airline and change, so verify current rules before you fly.**

## Keywords
packing list, travel packing, what to pack, carry-on packing, capsule wardrobe, packing cubes, travel light, packing tips, minimalist packing, trip checklist, luggage, packing techniques, business trip packing, beach trip, backpacking list, family travel packing

## Sample input
> "Pack me for 10 days — mixed city sightseeing and day hikes, temperate climate with some rain, carry-on only, laundry available once."

## Sample output
*(Illustrative shape.)*
- **Tailored list:** built from climate (layers + rain shell), duration with the laundry math (≈ a week of outfits + one wash, not 10 separate days), and activities (hiking shoes + city shoes, day pack).
- **Capsule wardrobe:** a tight color palette where a handful of tops × a few bottoms × layers yields dozens of outfits — travel-friendly, wrinkle-resistant fabrics.
- **Carry-on vs checked + technique:** medication, documents, valuables, and a change of clothes in the carry-on; rolling + packing cubes; shoes bagged with socks stuffed inside.
- **Essentials checklist** with the official-source redirect for entry/visa, health, baggage, and safety questions.

## Known limitations
- **Packing advice only** — it will not tell you whether you need a visa, what vaccines to get, whether a destination is safe, or what anything costs; it redirects you to the destination's government/embassy, a doctor or travel-health clinic, your airline, and official travel advisories.
- **Verify current rules** — airline baggage allowances + size/weight limits, carry-on liquid rules, and prohibited/restricted items vary by airline and security authority and change; confirm the current rules before you fly. It won't promise what security will allow through.
- **Lists are tailored starting points** — based on what you tell it (climate, activities); review and adjust for your trip.
- Not a visa/customs guide, health advisor, travel-booking, itinerary, or budget tool.
